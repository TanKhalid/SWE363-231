History
My role model and aspiration in this world is and will always be first and foremost, my mother. Reading used to be her hobby as a teenager before life and the resposibilities of being a mother took all of her time. Years later, her youngest child (myself) would grow to inherit that same passion for reading and literature as she once did. I have been reading english fiction since I was 10 years old and I almost immedietly fell in love with it. It has grown from a hobby into an integral part of my identity and my land of comfort.
